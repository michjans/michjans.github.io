    Planning of new Route Generator features

    A list of new features (ideas) that can be added in future versions of Route Generator (in random order). Since I'm developing on Route Generator in my spare time, there's no real planning for which features will be added when. Probably the chance of the appearances of new features will be higher in the winter period ;-).

    • Add multi language support (at least Dutch, besides English)
    • Make separate movies of chosen segments of the route
    • Add more scrolling methods, instead of just always keeping the vehicle centered.

    If you have suggestions for new features or other questions, please let me know.
