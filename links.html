Support for children in need

Nepal may seem a developed country when you are visiting as a tourist, but once you get away from the beaten (tourist) track, you will see it still needs a lot of development, especially related to women's and children's rights. Currently I am helping an organisation here in Nepal (VIN) to set-up a website to raise money for building an orphanage house. And in October I will join a charity trekking to the Mount Everest base camp to raise money for the oprhanage house as well.

If you also think it's important that unfortunate children are getting good education and a proper home, please have a look at the following sites and consider making a donation for children in need:
